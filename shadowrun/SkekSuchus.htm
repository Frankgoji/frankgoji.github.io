SkekSuchus (SkekSu or Su for short) appears to be a large bipedal crocodilian. In reality, he was born as a human, but experienced SURGE mutations in his adolescence that transformed him into the large reptilian he now is. He now ekes a living taking on various violent jobs, empowered by his adept practices and his arsenal of crossbow bolts and knives ("my other teeth" as he would call them).
DESCRIPTION Show: YESPage Break: NO
The family and friends that SkekSuchus grew up with were excellent navigators of all sorts of ships. As such, he primarily grew up around merchants, smugglers, and even pirates. Some of the people around him had a more humanitarian aspect and chose to help refugees pass borders by dodging the coast guard and private ocean enforcers.
SkekSuchus adored and even worshipped the ocean. However, several years ago, SkekSuchus began to be plagued with dreams and nightmares about the ocean. The dreams revolve around imagery of drowning, megalophobia, and thalassophobia. Of particular fixation is the imagery of enormous monsters that lie beneath the ocean's surface, that the vastness of the waters is soley to hide the vastness of the creatures below.
Despite their love of the ocean, SkekSuchus began to experience intense fear and even physical revulsion to the sea, and left his kin to go inland to work as a shadowrunner. SkekSuchus is constantly being divided by those opposing forces: genuine adoration and worship of the ocean, and mortal terror of it.
